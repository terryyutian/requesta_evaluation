Vocabulary Decision — Game Instructions

Objective

Decide as quickly and accurately as possible whether each letter string is a real English word.

How to Play

  • You will see 60 items, one at a time.
  • Click “Yes” if it is a real English word.
  • Click “No” if it is not a real English word.

Time & Results

  • You have 1 minute total. The timer starts when you press Start.
  • Try to complete as many items as you can while staying accurate.
  • Your completion count and accuracy rate will be reported during and after the game.

Ready?

If everything is clear, press Start to begin.
